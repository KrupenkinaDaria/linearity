Auto Trace Magic

Did you know you can turn any raster image into beautiful vector paths in Vectornator?

Our Auto Trace feature does the hard work for you. Here's how:

01 Use the Import tab to open your image, or just drag and drop the image onto the canvas from your files.
02 Select your image and press the Auto Trace button in the Inspector on the right of your screen.
03 Select your settings and choose between the three modes: Sketch, Photography, and Illustration.
04 Congratulations! Your image is now vector art. Use our tools to edit the paths, change the colors, and much more.
